Hi there! You've landed on my website, here you can find some basic information about me and the stuff that I spend my time on. I am a mathematician and statistician by training, now working on the probabilistic foundations of theoretical biology and epidemiology, in particular I study stochastic processes which are inspired by the evolution of species or the spread of diseases. When not thinking about such things, you'll find me with my brass band, reading a book or cooking a nice meal.

The most effective way for me to learn is to write. So I will strive to post small writings about what I am currently learning, what I enjoy doing or about some technicalities not profound enough to published anywhere else.
