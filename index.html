Frederik Mølkjær Andersen, PhD Fellow

I investigate the mathematics underlying evolution and disease spread, in particular through the lens of stochastic processes.
My research revolves around cutting down on the unrealistic assumptions on current models, and thus gaining a better understanding of our nature.

I'm an early career mathematical scientist with an ambition to do strong interdisciplinary research. Currently, I am doing my PhD at the Section of Epidemiology at the University of Copenhagen.
